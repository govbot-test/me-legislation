1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 32 MRSA §18375, sub-§1, ¶Q, as amended by PL 2023, c. 354, §8, is
3 further amended to read:
4 Q. Prescribe, dispense or administer fluoride, silver diamine fluoride, antimicrobial
5 solutions for mouth rinsing, topical anesthetics, other nonsystemic antimicrobial
6 agents, desensitizing agents and resorbable antimicrobial agents; and
7 Sec. 2. 32 MRSA §18375, sub-§1, ¶R, as enacted by PL 2023, c. 354, §8, is
8 amended to read:
9 R. Perform dental hygiene assessment, dental hygiene diagnosis and dental hygiene
10 treatment planning and implementation in the identification, prevention and
11 management of oral disease.; and
12 Sec. 3. 32 MRSA §18375, sub-§1, ¶S is enacted to read:
13 S. Administer local anesthesia or nitrous oxide analgesia, as long as the independent
14 practice dental hygienist has authority to administer the relevant medication pursuant
15 to section 18345, subsection 2, paragraph D or E, respectively.
16 SUMMARY
1177 This bill allows an independent practice dental hygienist to administer local anesthesia
1188 or nitrous oxide analgesia as long as the independent practice dental hygienist has the
1199 appropriate licensure authority.

Presented by Senator MOORE of Washington.
Printed on recycled paper
1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 34-B MRSA §3873-B, sub-§2, as enacted by PL 2021, c. 745, §1, is
3 amended to read:
4 2. Application of fund. The department shall use the money in the fund to reimburse
5 the legal costs incurred by private entities mental health care providers to initiate or renew
6 existing progressive treatment programs in accordance with section 3873‑A. A private
7 entity mental health care provider seeking reimbursement must submit to the department
8 an itemized bill of legal costs incurred to initiate the or renew a progressive treatment
9 program. The maximum amount the department may reimburse a private entity for the
10 legal costs to initiate a progressive treatment program is $800 The department may
11 reimburse mental health care providers for the legal costs to initiate or renew a progressive
12 treatment program, subject to available funds.
13 SUMMARY
1144 This bill allows mental health care providers to request reimbursement from the
1155 Department of Health and Human Services for both the initiation and renewal of
1166 progressive treatment programs, subject to available funds. Current law allows for
1177 reimbursement to private entities to initiate such programs.

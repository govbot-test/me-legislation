8 2. Interview. In filling any position in the classified service, the employing agency

11 SUMMARY
1122 This bill requires an employing agency, in filling a position in the classified service, to
1133 offer an interview to the spouse of a person who is serving on active duty in the United
1144 States Armed Forces including the Reserves of the United States Armed Forces and the
1155 National Guard if that spouse meets the minimum qualifications established for the
1166 position.

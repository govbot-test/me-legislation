132nd MAINE LEGISLATURE
FIRST REGULAR SESSION-2025
Legislative Document No. 984
S.P. 414 In Senate, March 11, 2025
An Act to Require Every State and Municipal Building to Have at
Least One Automated External Defibrillator
Reference to the Committee on State and Local Government suggested and ordered printed.
DAREK M. GRANT
Secretary of the Senate
Presented by Senator MOORE of Washington. (BY REQUEST)
Cosponsored by Representative ZAGER of Portland and
Representatives: DAIGLE of Fort Kent, GRIFFIN of Levant.
Printed on recycled paper

1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 5 MRSA §1755 is enacted to read:
3 §1755. Automated external defibrillators
4 The Department of Administrative and Financial Services, Bureau of General Services
5 shall ensure that each state building is equipped with at least one automated external
6 defibrillator. As used in this section, "automated external defibrillator" has the same
7 meaning as in Title 22, section 2150-C, subsection 1, paragraph A.
8 Sec. 2. 30-A MRSA §2007 is enacted to read:
9 §2007. Automated external defibrillators
10 A municipality shall ensure that each municipal building is equipped with at least one
11 automated external defibrillator. As used in this section, "automated external defibrillator"
12 has the same meaning as in Title 22, section 2150-C, subsection 1, paragraph A.
13 SUMMARY
1144 This bill directs the Department of Administrative and Financial Services, Bureau of
1155 General Services to ensure that each state building is equipped with at least one automated
1166 external defibrillator. It also directs each municipality to ensure that each municipal
1177 building is equipped with at least one automated external defibrillator.

1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 10 MRSA §9420, sub-§1, as enacted by PL 2011, c. 226, §1, is amended
3 by enacting a new first blocked paragraph to read:
4 For purposes of this subsection, "billing statement" means a monthly account statement
5 that a person is required to provide to a customer under federal law or regulation. This
6 subsection does not prohibit a person from charging a fee for providing a replacement
7 statement, a statement covering a custom date range or a statement requested for financial
8 or legal purposes or for any other labor-intensive or nonroutine requests to provide copies
9 of statements.
10 SUMMARY
1111 This bill clarifies that a person is not prohibited from charging a fee for providing a
1122 replacement billing statement, a statement covering a custom date range or a statement
1133 requested for financial or legal purposes or for any other labor-intensive or nonroutine
1144 requests to provide copies of statements.

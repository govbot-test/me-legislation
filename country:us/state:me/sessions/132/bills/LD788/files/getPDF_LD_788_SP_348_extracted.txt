3 §929-C. Research and development budgetary recommendations
4 The Maine Economic Growth Council, established in section 929‑A, with input from
5 the Office of Innovation, established pursuant to Title 5, section 13105, and the Maine
6 Innovation Economy Advisory Board, under section 949, shall review the innovation
7 economy action plan, as described in Title 10, chapter 107‑D, and develop specific annual
8 budgetary recommendations to support the plan’s vision and goals. These
9 recommendations must include specific bonding and General Fund appropriations
10 investment levels. By June 1st of each year, the council shall submit its recommendations,
11 along with an annual accountability update that summarizes the State’s commitment to
12 research and development investments in the prior year, to the Governor, the Legislature
13 and the joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over business,
14 research and economic development issues support and track progress toward the goal of
15 increasing the State's research and development spending as a percentage of the State's
16 gross domestic product to meet the national average by 2030 and maintaining that national
17 average each year thereafter. By December 1st of each year, the council shall submit an
18 annual accountability update that summarizes the State's commitment to research and
19 development investments in the prior year to the Governor and the joint standing committee
20 of the Legislature having jurisdiction over research and economic development matters and
21 the joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over appropriations and
22 financial affairs. In coordination with the Office of Innovation and the Maine Innovation
23 Economy Advisory Board, the council may also submit specific budgetary
24 recommendations to support this research and development spending goal.

1 Emergency preamble. Whereas, acts and resolves of the Legislature do not
2 become effective until 90 days after adjournment unless enacted as emergencies; and
3 Whereas, the cost of housing in the State has increased significantly over the past
4 several years; and
5 Whereas, residents of mobile home parks and manufactured housing communities are
6 among those most affected by the rising costs of housing in the State; and
7 Whereas, this legislation provides fee exemptions and tax incentives for certain
8 entities, including cooperative affordable housing corporations, beginning in 2026; and
9 Whereas, in the judgment of the Legislature, these facts create an emergency within
10 the meaning of the Constitution of Maine and require the following legislation as
11 immediately necessary for the preservation of the public peace, health and safety; now,
12 therefore,

15 SUMMARY
1166 This bill does the following.
17 1. It requires a person renewing a license to operate a manufactured housing
18 community to submit satisfactory evidence to the Manufactured Housing Board.
19 2. It updates the computation of taxable income of resident individuals and
20 corporations by providing a deduction in an amount equal to any gain recognized on the
21 sale by the taxpayer of an ownership interest greater than 50% in a qualified property if the
22 qualified property was transferred to a cooperative affordable housing corporation, a
23 municipal housing authority or an affiliate of a municipal housing authority.
24 3. It exempts certain entities that purchase a manufactured housing community or a
25 mobile home park from paying a transfer assessment fee and updates the sworn affirmation
26 of exemption requirement.
27 4. It establishes liability for damages for a person that is required to pay a transfer
28 assessment fee that fails to do so.
29 5. It moves from the Department of Professional and Financial Regulation to the Maine
30 State Housing Authority the rule-making authority governing the establishment of a
31 method to determine the net worth of an entity claiming an exemption from certain fees
32 related to the purchase of a mobile home park or a manufactured housing community.

3 SUMMARY
44 This bill transfers the supervision duties of the Chief of the Bureau of Capitol Police
55 within the Department of Public Safety to the Chief of the State Police and replaces the
66 position of Chief of the Bureau of Capitol Police with a State Police Lieutenant position.
77 The bill provides rule-making authority regarding security at the capitol area to a designee
88 of the Commissioner of Public Safety and requires that rules adopted governing security at
99 the legislative offices and other offices within the capitol complex include consultation
1100 with both the Commissioner of Public Safety and the Chief of the State Police. The bill
1111 also requires that the Chief of the State Police replace a Capitol Police officer position that
1122 is vacated with a state police officer position, contingent upon the funding of the
1133 reclassification of that position.

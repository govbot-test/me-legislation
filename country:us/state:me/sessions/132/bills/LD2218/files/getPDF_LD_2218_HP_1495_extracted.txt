5 SUMMARY
96 This bill is reported out by the Joint Standing Committee on Labor pursuant to the
107 Maine Revised Statutes, Title 3, section 955, subsection 4 to implement recommendations
118 related to the joint standing committee's review of the Maine Labor Relations Board under
129 the State Government Evaluation Act. The joint standing committee has not taken a
1130 position on the substance of this bill. By reporting this bill out, the joint standing committee
1141 is not suggesting and does not intend to suggest that it agrees or disagrees with any aspect
1152 of this bill; instead, the joint standing committee is reporting the bill out for the sole purpose
1163 of having a bill printed that can be referred to a joint standing committee for an appropriate
1174 public hearing and subsequent processing in the normal course. The joint standing
1185 committee is taking this action to ensure clarity and transparency in the legislative review
1196 of the proposals contained in the bill.
20 The bill increases the per diem rate for members of the Maine Labor Relations Board
21 from $75 per day to $150 per day. The bill also increases the per diem rate for the Chair of
22 the Maine Labor Relations Board from $100 per day to $200 per day.

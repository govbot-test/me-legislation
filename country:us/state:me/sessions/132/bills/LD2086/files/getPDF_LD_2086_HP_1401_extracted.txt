132nd MAINE LEGISLATURE
SECOND REGULAR SESSION-2026
Legislative Document No. 2086
An Act to Amend the Law Governing the Authority of the Governor
to Set Salary Rates for State Police Command Staff
Submitted by the Department of Public Safety pursuant to Joint Rule 203.
Reference to the Committee on Criminal Justice and Public Safety suggested and ordered
printed.
ROBERT B. HUNT
Clerk
Presented by Representative BUNKER of Farmington.
Printed on recycled paper
1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 25 MRSA §1504, first ¶, as amended by PL 1985, c. 785, Pt. B, §109, is
3 further amended to read:
4 The Governor shall determine the salary of the chief and, the deputy chief and majors.
5 The compensation of the other members of the State Police shall be is determined under
6 the Civil Service Law.
7 SUMMARY
88 This bill requires the Governor to determine the salaries of majors in the Department
99 of Public Safety, Maine State Police.

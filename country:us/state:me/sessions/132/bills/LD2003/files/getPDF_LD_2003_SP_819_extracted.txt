1 Emergency preamble. Whereas, acts and resolves of the Legislature do not
2 become effective until 90 days after adjournment unless enacted as emergencies; and
3 Whereas, the 90-day period may not terminate until after the beginning of the next
4 fiscal year; and
5 Whereas, emergency alert messaging on the statewide radio network operated by the
6 Maine Public Broadcasting Corporation benefits Maine residents in all 16 counties, and
7 state funding in this legislation will enable localization of public safety information and
8 emergency alert messaging on the statewide radio network; and
9 Whereas, in the judgment of the Legislature, these facts create an emergency within
10 the meaning of the Constitution of Maine and require the following legislation as
11 immediately necessary for the preservation of the public peace, health and safety; now,
12 therefore,

12 Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following:
13 'An Act to Reinforce Free Speech at Town Meetings by Requiring Opportunity for

15 Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and inserting the
16 following:
17 'Sec. 1. 30-A MRSA §2608 is enacted to read:
18 §2608. Public comment at public meetings of municipal officers
19 A reasonable opportunity for public comment must be provided on matters addressed
20 by the municipal governing body at any regularly scheduled public meeting of the
21 municipal officers. This section does not apply to a subcommittee meeting of a municipal
22 governing body. This section does not preempt or restrict the municipal governing body
23 from adopting and enforcing reasonable standards governing public comment, including
24 time limits and conduct standards.'

27 SUMMARY
2288 This amendment replaces the bill and changes the title. It requires that a reasonable
2299 opportunity for public comment must be provided on matters addressed by the municipal
3300 governing body at any regularly scheduled public meeting of the municipal officers. The
3311 amendment clarifies that this requirement does not apply to subcommittee meetings of a
3322 municipal governing body and does not preempt or restrict the municipal governing body
3333 from adopting and enforcing reasonable standards governing public comment, including
3344 time limits and conduct standards.

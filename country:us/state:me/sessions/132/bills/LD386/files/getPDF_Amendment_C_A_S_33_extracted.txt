12 Amend the bill by inserting after section 1 the following:
13 'Sec. 2. 20-A MRSA §1055, sub-§11, as amended by PL 2019, c. 525, §35, is
14 further amended to read:
15 11. Notification teams. Within 10 days after receiving notice from a district attorney
16 of an alleged juvenile offense or juvenile offense, pursuant to Title 15, section 3308‑B,
17 subsection 1 or after receiving notice from a law enforcement officer of credible
18 information that indicates an imminent danger to regarding the safety of students or school
19 personnel pursuant to Title 15, section 3301‑A, the superintendent shall convene a
20 notification team. The notification team must consist of the administrator of the school
21 building or the administrator's designee, at least one classroom teacher to whom the student
22 is assigned, a parent or guardian of the student and a guidance counselor. The notification
23 team is entitled to receive the information described in Title 15, section 3308‑B, subsection
24 1, paragraphs A to F and in Title 15, section 3301‑A. The notification team shall also
25 determine on the basis of need which school employees are entitled to receive that
26 information.
27 Confidentiality of this criminal justice information regarding juveniles must be ensured at
28 all times, and the information may be released only under the conditions of this subsection.
29 The superintendent shall ensure that confidentiality training is provided to all school
30 employees who have access to the information.'
31 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
32 number to read consecutively.

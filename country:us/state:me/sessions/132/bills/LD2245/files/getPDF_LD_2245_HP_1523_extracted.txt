1 Sec. 11. Authorization for reimbursement of costs associated with contract
2 resolution. The Department of Administrative and Financial Services may be reimbursed
3 from the Salary Plan program, General Fund account in the Department of Administrative
4 and Financial Services for the costs of contract resolution, administration and

8 chapter 492 apply retroactively to September 24, 2025.
9 SUMMARY
1100 This bill implements the cost items in collective bargaining agreements reached
1111 between the State and the Maine Service Employees Association and provides for equitable
1122 treatment for confidential employees and certain other employees excluded from collective
1133 bargaining.
14 The bill specifies the costs from the General Fund and Highway Fund to fund salary
15 increases and authorizes the transfer by financial order of available General Fund and
16 Highway Fund balances as necessary.
17 The bill provides for the adjustment of certain salary schedules in fiscal year 2026-27
18 consistent with agreements ratified by the Maine Service Employees Association by
19 August 31, 2026. The bill provides for similar and equitable treatment of confidential
20 employees, probationary employees and other employees excluded from collective
21 bargaining.
22 The bill amends Public Law 2025, chapter 492 to remove the Maine Service Employees
23 Association from the provision in that chapter regarding the requirement that salary
24 schedules must be adjusted consistent with agreements ratified by December 31, 2025 and
25 the provision in that chapter regarding costs to the General Fund and Highway Fund. The
26 bill makes those changes retroactive to the effective date of chapter 492.

15 Sec. C-1. Funding for free volunteer tax assistance. On or before June 30, 2027,
16 the State Controller shall transfer $50,000 from the unappropriated surplus of the General
17 Fund to the All Other line category in the University of Maine System, New Ventures
18 Maine program, Other Special Revenue Funds account to support a coalition of
19 approximately 50 organizations providing free volunteer tax assistance and other financial
20 education services to low-income individuals and families statewide.
21 Sec. C-2. Report. No later than December 1, 2027, the Chancellor of the University
22 of Maine System, in maintaining the so-called New Ventures Maine program pursuant to
23 the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 20-A, section 10922, shall submit a report to the joint
24 standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over taxation matters that
25 includes a description of how the coalition receiving the funds transferred pursuant to
26 section 1 of this Part is using those funds, how many individuals and families the coalition
27 has served and how many individuals the coalition assisted in filing their taxes with those
28 funds.

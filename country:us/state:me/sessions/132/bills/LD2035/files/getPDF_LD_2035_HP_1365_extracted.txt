Section 1:
132nd MAINE LEGISLATURE
SECOND REGULAR SESSION-2026
Legislative Document No. 2035
An Act to Increase the Fee Cap for the Board of Licensing of
Auctioneers
Submitted by the Department of Professional and Financial Regulation pursuant to Joint
Rule 203.
Received by the Clerk of the House on December 3, 2025. Referred to the Committee on
Labor pursuant to Joint Rule 308.2 and ordered printed pursuant to Joint Rule 401.
ROBERT B. HUNT
Clerk
Presented by Representative ROEDER of Bangor.
Printed on recycled paper
1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 32 MRSA §287-A, as enacted by PL 2001, c. 323, §15 and amended by PL
3 2011, c. 286, Pt. B, §5, is amended to read:
4 §287-A. Fees
5 The Director of the Office of Professional and Occupational Regulation within the
6 Department of Professional and Financial Regulation may establish by rule fees for
7 purposes authorized under this chapter in amounts that are reasonable and necessary for
8 their respective purposes, except that the fee for any one purpose may not exceed $200
9 $400 annually. Rules adopted pursuant to this section are routine technical rules pursuant
10 to Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter II‑A 2-A.
11 SUMMARY
1122 This bill increases the cap on fees set by the Director of the Office of Professional and
1133 Occupational Regulation within the Department of Professional and Financial Regulation
1144 to maintain the operations of the Board of Licensing of Auctioneers.

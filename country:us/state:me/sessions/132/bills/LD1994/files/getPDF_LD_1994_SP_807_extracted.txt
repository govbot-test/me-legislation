Submitted by the Judicial Department pursuant to Joint Rule 203.

DAREK M. GRANT
Secretary of the Senate
Presented by Senator CARNEY of Cumberland.
Printed on recycled paper
1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 4 MRSA §161, as amended by PL 1995, c. 388, §1 and affected by §8, is
3 further amended by amending the section headnote to read:
4 §161. Justice of the peace; appointment; duties; salary; immunity
5 Sec. 2. 4 MRSA §161, as amended by PL 1995, c. 388, §1 and affected by §8, is
6 further amended by enacting after the 3rd paragraph a new paragraph to read:
7 A person authorized to serve as a justice of the peace is immune from any civil liability,
8 to the same extent as employees of governmental entities under the Maine Tort Claims Act,
9 for acts performed within the scope of the person's justice of the peace duties.
10 SUMMARY
1111 This bill provides immunity for justices of the peace under the Maine Tort Claims Act
1122 for acts performed within the scope of their justice of the peace duties.

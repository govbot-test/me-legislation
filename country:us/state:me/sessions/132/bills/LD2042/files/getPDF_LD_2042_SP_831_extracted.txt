1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 30-A MRSA §2007 is enacted to read:
3 §2007. Public notice
4 Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, a municipality required by this
5 Title to place a notice in a newspaper may meet this requirement by placing the notice on
6 its publicly accessible website, as long as the notice on the website contains all information
7 required to be published in a newspaper.
8 SUMMARY
99 This bill provides an alternative way for a municipality to provide public notice as
1100 required in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 30-A. In place of notice in a newspaper, a
1111 municipality may choose to provide the required notice on its publicly accessible website.

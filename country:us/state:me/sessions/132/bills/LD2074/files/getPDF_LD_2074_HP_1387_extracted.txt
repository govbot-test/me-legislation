18 SUMMARY
1199 This bill changes the licensure requirements for social workers. The bill enacts
2200 definitions of conditional licensure categories, identifies qualifying degrees for licensure,
2211 streamlines the qualifications for licensure specific to consultation and social work
2222 experience requirements, removes requirements for initial licensure that have created
2233 barriers to entering or re-entering the social work profession, clarifies the consultant
2244 eligibility, removes the 2-tiered system for eligibility for Department of Health and Human
2255 Services social workers and social workers who are not employees of the department and
2266 expands the State Board of Social Worker Licensure's authority to consider consultation
2277 and social work experience gained in this State or another jurisdiction when seeking initial
2288 licensure, late renewal or reinstatement of licensure.

18 SUMMARY
1199 This bill changes the licensure requirements for social workers. The bill enacts
2200 definitions of conditional licensure categories, identifies qualifying degrees for licensure,
2211 streamlines the qualifications for licensure specific to consultation and social work
2222 experience requirements, removes requirements for initial licensure that have created
2233 barriers to entering or re-entering the social work profession, clarifies the consultant
2244 eligibility, removes the 2-tiered system for eligibility for Department of Health and Human
2255 Services social workers and social workers who are not employees of the department and
2266 expands the State Board of Social Worker Licensure's authority to consider consultation
2277 and social work experience gained in this State or another jurisdiction when seeking initial
2288 licensure, late renewal or reinstatement of licensure.

2288 a game or contest in which 2 or more contestants, none of which may be the fantasy contest
2299 operator, pay an entry fee to participate. The amendment removes the definition of
3300 "proposition bet" from the bill and the prohibition on fantasy contests based on proposition
3311 betting. The amendment removes from the bill changes to the initial and renewal fee for a
3322 license for a fantasy contest operator and removes the repeal of the provision of law
3333 allowing a fantasy contest operator to operate a fantasy contest while an initial license,
3344 license renewal or license transfer is pending. The amendment also provides that the

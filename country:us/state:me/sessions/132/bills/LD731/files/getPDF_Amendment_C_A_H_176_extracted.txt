9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 473, L.D. 731, “An Act to Prohibit
10 Municipalities from Barring the Creation of Homeless Shelters”
11 Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and inserting the
12 following:
13 'Sec. 1. 30-A MRSA §4357-B is enacted to read:
14 §4357-B. Homeless shelters
15 1. Definition. As used in this section, "homeless shelter" means a housing facility,
16 the primary purpose of which is to provide temporary overnight accommodations to
17 persons experiencing homelessness who are guests in either a dormitory-style or dwelling-
18 unit-style arrangement or any combination of such arrangements.
19 2. Homeless shelters may not be prohibited. A municipality may not prohibit
20 homeless shelters in the municipality. This prohibition does not limit a municipality's home
21 rule authority to reasonably regulate homeless shelters.'
22 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
23 number to read consecutively.

1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 7 MRSA §2, sub-§6, as amended by PL 2025, c. 309, §§2 to 4, is further
3 amended by enacting at the end a new last blocked paragraph to read:
4 The commissioner shall, with the input of the advisory committee and in partnership with
5 outside agencies or other state agencies as necessary, institute an annual survey and
6 regularly publish a report regarding food insecurity in the State. The survey may include
7 measurements of disparities of food insecurity in social subgroups, including those defined
8 by age, geography, income, race and veteran status.
9 SUMMARY
1100 This bill directs the Commissioner of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry, in
1111 partnership with outside agencies or other state agencies as necessary, to institute, with
1122 input of the commissioner's advisory committee to guide the implementation of the State's
1133 strategic plan to end hunger in the State by 2030, an annual survey and regularly publish a
1144 report regarding food insecurity in the State.

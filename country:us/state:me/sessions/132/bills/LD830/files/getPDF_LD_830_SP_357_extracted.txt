1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 38 MRSA §484, sub-§11 is enacted to read:
3 11. Special provisions; solar energy development. In the case of a solar energy
4 development, the proposed development will be surrounded by a barrier, which may
5 include, but is not limited to, a fence, trees, bushes or other vegetation. The barrier must be
6 designed to sufficiently conceal the solar energy development from view from abutting
7 properties, as determined by the department.
8 SUMMARY
99 This bill amends the site location of development laws to require that, prior to
1100 approving a solar energy development proposal, the Department of Environmental
1111 Protection find that the proposed development will be surrounded by a barrier designed to
1122 sufficiently conceal the development from view from abutting properties.

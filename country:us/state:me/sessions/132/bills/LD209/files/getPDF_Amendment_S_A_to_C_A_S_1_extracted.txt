1 L.D. 209
2 Date: (Filing No. S- )
4 STATE OF MAINE
5 SENATE
6 132ND LEGISLATURE
7 FIRST REGULAR SESSION
8 SENATE AMENDMENT “ ” to COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “A” to H.P. 131,
9 L.D. 209, “An Act to Make Supplemental Appropriations and Allocations from the General
10 Fund and Other Funds for the Expenditures of State Government and to Change Certain
11 Provisions of the Law Necessary to the Proper Operations of State Government for the
12 Fiscal Year Ending June 30, 2025”
13 Amend the amendment by inserting after Part X the following:
14 'PART Y
15 Sec. Y-1. Cost-of-living adjustment for essential support workers. The
16 Department of Health and Human Services shall seek approval from the United States
17 Department of Health and Human Services, Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services
18 to implement a cost-of-living adjustment of 1.95% in fiscal year 2024-25 for
19 reimbursement rates for essential support workers pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes,
20 Title 22, section 7402. The department shall seek approval under this section as soon as
21 practicable and, upon receipt of the approval, the department shall make the cost-of-living
22 adjustment retroactive to the effective date approved by the Centers for Medicare and
23 Medicaid Services.'
24 Amend the amendment by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or
25 section number to read consecutively.
26 SUMMARY
2277 This amendment requires the Department of Health and Human Services to seek
2288 approval from the United States Department of Health and Human Services, Centers for
2299 Medicare and Medicaid Services to implement a cost-of-living adjustment of 1.95% in
3300 fiscal year 2024-25 for reimbursement rates for essential support workers.

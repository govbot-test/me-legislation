1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 24-A MRSA §4349, sub-§7 is enacted to read:
3 7. Covered persons using mail order pharmacy. If a covered person is using a mail
4 order pharmacy, the pharmacy benefits manager shall allow for the dispensing of the
5 covered person's prescriptions at a network pharmacy under the following circumstances
6 to ensure that the covered person has access to prescription drugs:
7 A. If the prescription is delayed by more than one day after the expected delivery date
8 provided by the mail order pharmacy; or
9 B. If the prescription drug arrives in an unusable condition.
10 SUMMARY
1111 This bill requires pharmacy benefits managers to allow for the dispensing of a covered
1122 person's prescriptions at a network pharmacy if the prescription is delayed by more than
1133 one day after the expected delivery date provided by a mail order pharmacy or if the
1144 prescription drug arrives in an unusable condition.

1 Emergency preamble. Whereas, acts and resolves of the Legislature do not
2 become effective until 90 days after adjournment unless enacted as emergencies; and
3 Whereas, current law prohibits a distribution of funds from the Stipend Fund that
4 takes into account prizes offered and paid by a licensee at an agricultural fair competition
5 or event held other than during the period in which the fair is held; and
6 Whereas, changing the law to allow distributions from the fund to apply to fair
7 competitions and events that occur during a 24-hour window prior to the period in which
8 the fair is held would allow judges of fair competitions and events to volunteer in other
9 capacities during the period in which the fair is held; and
10 Whereas, this change needs to take effect as soon as possible to ensure that it will
11 apply to the upcoming fair season; and
12 Whereas, in the judgment of the Legislature, these facts create an emergency within
13 the meaning of the Constitution of Maine and require the following legislation as
14 immediately necessary for the preservation of the public peace, health and safety; now,
15 therefore,
16 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
17 Sec. 1. 7 MRSA §86, sub-§5, as amended by PL 2025, c. 161, §3, is further
18 amended by amending the first blocked paragraph to read:
19 In determining distribution under this subsection, an allowance may not be made on
20 premiums offered and paid by a licensee at any competition or event held other than during
21 the period in which its annual fair is held judged up to 24 hours prior to the licensed fair
22 dates or during the licensed fair dates. Allowance may not be made or consideration given
23 for lump sums, payments or premiums previously arranged and agreed upon for the
24 presentation and display of any animals or products without regard to competition or event.
25 Emergency clause. In view of the emergency cited in the preamble, this legislation
26 takes effect when approved.
27 SUMMARY
2288 This bill amends the law governing the distribution of funds from the Stipend Fund to
2299 agricultural fair licensees by allowing prizes paid for a competition or event judged up to
3300 24 hours prior to the licensed fair dates to be considered in calculating the distribution.

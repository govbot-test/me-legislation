1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 9-A MRSA §3-308, sub-§1, as repealed and replaced by PL 1985, c. 113,
3 §2, is repealed and the following enacted in its place:
4 1. Except as provided in this section, a creditor may not contract for or receive
5 payments of principal and interest pursuant to a schedule of payments under which any one
6 payment is not substantially equal to all other payments, excluding:
7 A. A down payment receivable by the creditor;
8 B. A transaction pursuant to a schedule of payments under which the intervals between
9 any consecutive payments differ substantially; and
10 C. A simple interest transaction pursuant to a schedule of payments under which the
11 scheduled payments are not substantially equal but no payment is more than 50%
12 greater than any other payment;
13 SUMMARY
1144 This bill excludes payments made pursuant to a simple interest transaction that are no
1155 more than 50% greater than any other payment from the law requiring that consumer credit
1166 payments pursuant to a schedule be substantially equal to all other payments.

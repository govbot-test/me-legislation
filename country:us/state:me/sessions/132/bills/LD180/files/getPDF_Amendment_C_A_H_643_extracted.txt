25 SUMMARY
2266 This amendment replaces the bill and changes the title. The amendment prohibits a
2277 health insurance carrier, or a pharmacy benefits manager under contract with a carrier, from
2288 reimbursing a pharmacy provider for a prescription drug or pharmacy service in an amount
2299 that is less than the amount the carrier or pharmacy benefits manager reimburses a
3300 pharmacy provider affiliated with the carrier or pharmacy benefits manager for the same
3311 prescription drug or pharmacy service.

132nd MAINE LEGISLATURE
FIRST REGULAR SESSION-2025
Legislative Document No. 771
S.P. 329 In Senate, February 25, 2025
An Act to Include Possession of Compounds, Mixtures or
Substances Containing a Detectable Amount of Certain Scheduled
Drugs to Prove the Offense of Unlawful Trafficking of Scheduled
Drugs
Reference to the Committee on Criminal Justice and Public Safety suggested and ordered
printed.
DAREK M. GRANT
Secretary of the Senate
Presented by Senator BALDACCI of Penobscot.
Printed on recycled paper

1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 17-A MRSA §1101, sub-§27 is enacted to read:
3 27. "Amphetamine" means any compound, mixture or preparation containing
4 amphetamine in any quantity.
5 Sec. 2. 17-A MRSA §1101, sub-§28 is enacted to read:
6 28. "Methamphetamine" means any compound, mixture or preparation containing
7 methamphetamine in any quantity.
8 SUMMARY
99 This bill amends the Maine Criminal Code to define "amphetamine" to mean any
1100 compound, mixture or preparation containing amphetamine in any quantity and
1111 "methamphetamine" to mean any compound, mixture or preparation containing
1122 methamphetamine in any quantity.

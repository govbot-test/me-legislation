8 HOUSE AMENDMENT “ ” to COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “A” to H.P. 377,
9 L.D. 609, “An Act Making Certain Appropriations and Allocations and Changing Certain
10 Provisions of Law Necessary to the Proper Operations of State Government”
11 Amend the amendment by inserting after Part TT the following:
12 'PART UU
13 Sec. UU-1. State employees required to pay premiums for paid family and
14 medical leave. Notwithstanding any provision of the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 26,
15 chapter 7, subchapter 6-C to the contrary, an employee of State Government, including an
16 employee in the executive, legislative or judicial branch and a state department or agency,
17 is responsible for paying the employee's share of the premium imposed pursuant to Title
18 26, section 850-F, subsection 3 until the State receives approval for a private plan pursuant
19 to Title 26, section 850-H and the State is no longer required to pay the premium.
20 Sec. UU-2. Appropriations and allocations. The following appropriations and
21 allocations are made.
22 ADMINISTRATIVE AND FINANCIAL SERVICES, DEPARTMENT OF
23 Departments and Agencies - Statewide 0016
24 Initiative: Deallocates funds representing savings to the Highway Fund as a result of the
25 requirement that state employees contribute one-half of the 1% assessment on employers
26 for the paid family and medical leave benefits program.

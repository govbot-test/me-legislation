4 STATE OF MAINE
5 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
6 132ND LEGISLATURE
7 FIRST REGULAR SESSION
8 HOUSE AMENDMENT “ ” to COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “A” to H.P. 377,
9 L.D. 609, “An Act Making Certain Appropriations and Allocations and Changing Certain
10 Provisions of Law Necessary to the Proper Operations of State Government”
11 Amend the amendment by inserting after Part TT the following:
12 'PART UU
13 Sec. UU-1. 20-A MRSA §14 is enacted to read:
14 §14. Prohibited participation of biological males in female sports; prohibited access
15 to locker rooms and restrooms designated for female use
16 An institution in the State that allows a biological male to participate in a sport
17 designated for female-only participation or allows a biological male to use a locker room
18 or restroom designated for female use may not receive funding from the State. For purposes
19 of this section, "biological male" refers to an individual's physical form as a male based
20 solely on the individual's reproductive biology and genetics at birth.'
21 Amend the amendment by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or
22 section number to read consecutively.
23 SUMMARY
2244 This amendment prohibits institutions that allow a biological male to participate in a
2255 sport designated for female-only participation or allow a biological male to use a locker
2266 room or restroom designated for female use from receiving funding from the State.

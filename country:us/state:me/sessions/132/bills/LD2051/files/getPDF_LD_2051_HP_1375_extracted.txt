1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 22 MRSA §3104-A, sub-§5 is enacted to read:
3 5. Definition. As used in this section, "noncitizen legally admitted to the United
4 States" includes, without limitation, any person who:
5 A. Is pursuing any lawful immigration process;
6 B. Has permanent residence in the United States under color of law; or
7 C. Was eligible to receive Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program benefits as of
8 July 3, 2025 but is no longer eligible for federally funded food assistance benefits
9 pursuant to the federal One Big Beautiful Bill Act, Public Law 119-21 (2025).
10 SUMMARY
1111 This bill provides that in the laws governing the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance
1122 Program, the term "noncitizen legally admitted to the United States" includes, without
1133 limitation, any person who is pursuing any lawful immigration process, who has permanent
1144 residence in the United States under color of law or who was eligible to receive
1155 Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program benefits as of July 3, 2025 but is no longer
1166 eligible for federally funded food assistance benefits pursuant to the federal One Big
1177 Beautiful Bill Act.

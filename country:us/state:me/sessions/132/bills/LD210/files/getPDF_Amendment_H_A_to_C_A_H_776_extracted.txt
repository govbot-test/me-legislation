15 Sec. BBBB-1. 22 MRSA §1708-A is enacted to read:
16 §1708-A. Per diem payments to Maine Veterans' Homes
17 Services provided by a veterans' facility must be reimbursed by the MaineCare program
18 at rates established by the department in accordance with this section. This section applies
19 to rates that are paid from all funds, including federal funds, by an agency of the State to a
20 veterans' facility for resident care.
21 1. Definitions. As used in this section, unless the context otherwise indicates, the
22 following terms have the following meanings.
23 A. "Multilevel health care facility" has the same meaning as in section 7852,
24 subsection 10-A.
25 B. "Nursing home" has the same meaning as in section 1812-A.
26 C. "Residential care facility" has the same meaning as in section 7852, subsection 14.
27 D. "Veterans' facility" means a nursing home, residential care facility or multilevel
28 health care facility that is operated by the Maine Veterans' Homes at any location
29 required under Title 37-B, section 601, subsection 2.
30 2. Per resident basis. Veterans' facilities must be reimbursed on a per resident daily
31 rate basis for services to residents covered by the MaineCare program.
32 3. Rate calculations. Different rates must be established for nursing home services
33 and residential care facility services provided by a veterans' facility.
34 A. For nursing homes, rate calculations under this section must follow the process and
35 principles established in section 3173-J to determine a daily rate that is reasonable and

316 adequate to cover the MaineCare allowable costs of operating a nursing home operated

349 components of the daily rate. Adjustments to rates and reimbursement for purposes

472 established in department rule.
8 B. For residential care facilities, rate calculations under this section must include an
9 add-on per diem rate that is reasonable and adequate to cover the demonstrated
10 MaineCare allowable costs of operating veterans' facilities that are residential care
11 facilities when provided in addition to the per diem rates already established under
12 MaineCare rules for reimbursement of residential care facilities. The add-on amounts
13 are subject to cost settlement so that total reimbursement does not exceed MaineCare
14 allowable costs for each facility.
15 4. Annual adjustments for inflation. The per diem rates must be annually adjusted
16 for inflation to the same extent that MaineCare reimbursement rates for other nursing
17 homes and residential care facilities that are not operated by the Maine Veterans' Homes
18 are adjusted.
19 Sec. BBBB-2. Supplemental payments previously appropriated. Upon
20 implementation of the rate calculations process established in the Maine Revised Statutes,
21 Title 22, section 1708-A, any supplemental funding allocated or appropriated for the Maine
22 Veterans' Homes in Public Law 2023, chapter 412 that has not been expended may not be
23 used as supplemental payments but may be used for the purposes of this Part.

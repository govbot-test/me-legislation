14 'PART BBBB
15 Sec. BBBB-1. 22-A MRSA §214, sub-§1, ¶A-1 is enacted to read:
16 A-1. "In good standing" means that a service provider has successfully complied with
17 the terms and conditions of its most recent contract with the department and has no
18 unresolved or outstanding compliance concerns.
19 Sec. BBBB-2. 22-A MRSA §214, sub-§1, ¶C is enacted to read:
20 C. "Previous contract rate" means the payment rate or structure established under the
21 most recent contract between a service provider and the department.
22 Sec. BBBB-3. 22-A MRSA §214, sub-§1, ¶D is enacted to read:
23 D. "Service provider" means an entity, including, but not limited to, a private or
24 nonprofit behavioral health agency, housing assistance provider or other nonprofit
25 organization that contracts with the department to provide services.
26 Sec. BBBB-4. 22-A MRSA §214, sub-§5 is enacted to read:
27 5. Contract extensions. The department shall include language in its contracts with
28 service providers providing that, in the event of a delay in the finalization of a
29 noncompetitively procured contract renewal, as long as the delays are solely the result of
30 department or state administrative procurement processes, the value of the renewal contract
31 meets the review threshold of the State Procurement Review Committee as set forth in Title
32 5, section 1824-B and the service provider remains in good standing with the department,
33 the department must offer the service provider the option of extending the contract for up
34 to 6 months at the previous contract rate and terms until the renewal of the contract is
35 finalized.

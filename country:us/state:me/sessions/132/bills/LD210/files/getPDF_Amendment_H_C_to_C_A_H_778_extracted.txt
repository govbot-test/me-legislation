15 Sec. BBBB-1. 20-A MRSA §15699 is enacted to read:
16 §15699. Fund for Public Prekindergarten to Grade 12 Education
17 1. Fund established. The Fund for Public Prekindergarten to Grade 12 Education,
18 referred to in this section as "the fund," is established as an interest-bearing account
19 administered by the department.
20 2. Revenue. The Treasurer of State shall deposit all revenue collected pursuant to Title
21 36, section 5111, subsection 7 from the income tax surcharge to fund education into the
22 fund according to the schedule in Title 36, section 5111, subsection 7. All interest earned
23 by the fund becomes part of the fund.
24 3. Use of fund. The commissioner shall use the full amount of revenue deposited to
25 the fund to fund public prekindergarten to grade 12 education. Revenue deposited to the
26 fund must be used as additional funds for public prekindergarten to grade 12 education and
27 may not be used in place of funds appropriated or allocated to meet the State's annual target
28 share pursuant to section 15671. The funds must be allocated in the same manner that the
29 state share under section 15671 is allocated.

26 Sec. BBBB-3. 36 MRSA §5111, sub-§7 is enacted to read:
27 7. Income tax surcharge to fund education. An income tax surcharge is established
28 and administered as follows.
29 A. For tax years beginning on or after January 1, 2026, in addition to any other tax
30 imposed by this chapter, a surcharge at the rate of 2% is imposed on that portion of the
31 taxpayer's Maine taxable income in excess of:
32 (1) For single individuals and married persons filing separate returns, $1,000,000;
33 (2) For heads of households, $1,500,000; and
34 (3) For individuals filing married joint returns or surviving spouses, $2,000,000.
35 B. All revenue collected under this subsection must be used to fund public
36 prekindergarten to grade 12 education and must be deposited each year into the Fund
37 for Public Prekindergarten to Grade 12 Education established in Title 20-A, section
38 15699 in accordance with this paragraph.
39 (1) Prior to January 1st of each year, the bureau shall estimate the annual revenue
40 to be collected from the surcharge for the immediately following calendar year.
41 (2) On the first of each month, beginning on January 1, 2026, the Treasurer of State
42 shall deposit 8.333% of the estimated annual total revenue from the surcharge into
43 the Fund for Public Prekindergarten to Grade 12 Education.

1 SUMMARY
22 This amendment imposes an income tax surcharge of 2% on that portion of a taxpayer's
33 Maine taxable income in excess of $1,000,000 for single filers and married persons filing
44 separate returns, $1,500,000 for heads of households and $2,000,000 for married persons
55 filing jointly or surviving spouses to fund public prekindergarten to grade 12 education and
66 exempts the revenue from the surcharge from the calculation of state-municipal revenue
77 sharing. The amendment establishes the Fund for Public Prekindergarten to Grade 12
88 Education and requires the Treasurer of State, beginning January 1, 2026, to deposit
99 monthly into the fund 8.333% of the estimated annual total revenue from the surcharge.
1100 Revenue transferred into the fund must be used to fund public prekindergarten to grade 12
1111 education in addition to, but not in place of, funds appropriated or allocated to meet the
1122 State's annual target for education funding. The amendment also adds an appropriations
1133 and allocations section.

1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 36 MRSA §5219-WW, sub-§3, as enacted by PL 2019, c. 555, §6, is
3 amended to read:
4 3. Maximum credit; carry-forward. The total credit amount available pursuant to
5 this section and section 2534 to be allocated by the authority for each calendar year
6 beginning on or after January 1, 2021 and ending on or before December 31, 2028 is subject
7 to the following limitations.
8 A. The total allocation may not exceed $10,000,000. Any portion of that amount not
9 allocated in a calendar year may be carried forward and available to be allocated in
10 subsequent calendar years, except that:
11 (1) Any previously allocated credits returned to the authority, excluding any
12 credits recaptured under subsection 7, must be added to that amount; and
13 (2) The authority may not allocate more than $15,000,000 in any calendar year.
14 B. No more than 20% of credits allocated in any calendar year may be allocated under
15 subsection 2, paragraph B.
16 C. Ten percent of credits first available to be allocated in any calendar year must be
17 set aside to be allocated for the purpose of qualified rural development preservation
18 projects pursuant to subsection 2, paragraph B. Any portion of the amount under this
19 paragraph not allocated in a calendar year must be carried forward and be available to
20 be allocated in subsequent calendar years for the purpose of qualified rural
21 development preservation projects. To the extent that any amounts set aside under this
22 paragraph are not allocated on or before December 31, 2028, those amounts may be
23 allocated by the authority without regard to whether the project is a qualified rural
24 development preservation project.
25 D. Only those credits that have been carried forward or returned, excluding any credits
26 recaptured under subsection 7, as described in this subsection may be allocated by the
27 authority after December 31, 2028.
28 SUMMARY
2299 This bill removes the provision of law that terminates the income tax credit for
3300 affordable housing on December 31, 2028.

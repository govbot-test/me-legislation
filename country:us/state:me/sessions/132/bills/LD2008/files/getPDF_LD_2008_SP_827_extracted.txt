1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 20-A MRSA §5805, sub-§3-A is enacted to read:
3 3-A. Maine School of Science and Mathematics. A sending unit that does not
4 operate or contract with a secondary school shall pay the cost of attendance, including room
5 and board, for a student in that sending unit to attend the Maine School of Science and
6 Mathematics. The maximum allowable cost of attendance at the Maine School of Science
7 and Mathematics is the actual room and board rate determined by the Board of Trustees of
8 the Maine School of Science and Mathematics or the state average per public secondary
9 student cost as adjusted, whichever is lower, multiplied by the local contribution percentage
10 of the essential programs and services amounts, as determined by the most recent report of
11 the Department of Education, for that sending unit. The school board of the sending unit
12 may vote to pay a higher cost of attendance rate.

1 SUMMARY
52 This bill requires a sending school administrative unit that does not operate or contract
63 with a secondary school to pay the cost of attending the Maine School of Science and
74 Mathematics for students from the unit and specifies that the cost of attending the Maine
85 School of Science and Mathematics includes room and board. The bill also provides
96 funding to the Maine School of Science and Mathematics to support administrative
107 operations, to support in-state students' room and board expenses and for infrastructure
118 upgrades to the school's dormitory.

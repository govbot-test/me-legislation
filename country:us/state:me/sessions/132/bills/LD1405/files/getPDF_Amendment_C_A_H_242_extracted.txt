15 SUMMARY
1166 This amendment replaces the bill and changes the title. It limits an intervenor or a
1177 participant seeking funding to participate in a Public Utilities Commission adjudicatory
1188 proceeding or nonadjudicatory proceeding to an individual acting in a personal capacity
1199 rather than in a business or professional capacity. It requires the commission to adopt rules
2200 that include the method by which the commission will allow a group of more than one
2211 individual to seek and qualify for intervenor or participant funding jointly, as long as each
2222 individual is acting in a personal capacity rather than in a business or professional capacity.

1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 22 MRSA §3477, sub-§2, as amended by PL 2003, c. 653, §10, is further
3 amended to read:
4 2. Reports. Reports regarding abuse, neglect or exploitation must be made
5 immediately by telephone to the department either by telephone or by an online portal
6 designated by the department and must be followed by a written report within 48 hours if
7 requested by the department. The reports must contain the name and address of the
8 involved adult; information regarding the nature and extent of the abuse, neglect or
9 exploitation; the source of the report; and the name of the person making the report;, that
10 person's occupation; and where that person can be contacted. The report may contain any
11 other information that the reporter believes may be helpful.
12 SUMMARY
1133 Current law requires that reports to the Department of Health and Human Services
1144 regarding abuse, neglect or exploitation of adults must be made by telephone. This bill
1155 adds the option of reporting by an online portal to align state law with federal regulations
1166 promulgated in June 2024 that require states to allow at least 2 methods of reporting, one
1177 of which must be online.

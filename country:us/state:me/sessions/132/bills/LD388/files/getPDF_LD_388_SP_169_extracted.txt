from State Licensing Requirements

1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 22 MRSA §8301-A, sub-§10 is enacted to read:
3 10. Exemption from duplicative licensure. Notwithstanding any provision of this
4 chapter to the contrary, a child care provider on a military installation or a facility licensed
5 or certified as a family child care provider by the United States Department of Defense or
6 by the United States Coast Guard is exempt from the requirements of this chapter.
7 SUMMARY
88 This bill exempts a child care provider on a military installation or a facility licensed
99 or certified as a family child care provider by the United States Department of Defense or
1100 by the United States Coast Guard from the requirements of the Maine Revised Statutes,
1111 Title 22, chapter 1673, which governs the licensing of child care facilities.

1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 12 MRSA §11154, sub-§14, ¶D, as enacted by PL 2013, c. 538, §24, is
3 amended to read:
4 D. A permit allocated under this subsection may be used only for the year, season, sex
5 and wildlife management district for which the permit is issued. A deferment may not
6 be granted under subsection 17 or 18 for a permit allocated under this subsection.
7 Sec. 2. 12 MRSA §11154, sub-§14, ¶H is enacted to read:
8 H. A recipient of a moose permit granted under this subsection may not sell a
9 subpermittee or an alternate subpermitee designation.
10 SUMMARY
1111 This bill prohibits a hunting outfitter from deferring a moose hunting permit due to
1122 significant medical illness or due to a single household's holding multiple permits. The bill
1133 also clarifies that a recipient of a moose permit from a hunting outfitter may not sell a
1144 subpermittee or an alternate subpermittee designation.

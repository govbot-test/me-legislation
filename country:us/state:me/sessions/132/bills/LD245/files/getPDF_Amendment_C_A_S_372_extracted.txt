1 1. It requires rather than authorizes a municipality to adopt a plan stipulating the
2 method by which transporting emergency medical services will be delivered within the
3 municipality.
4 2. It removes from the bill the provision that amends the Maine Emergency Medical
5 Services Act of 1982 to authorize an emergency medical services provider to render
6 emergency medical services within a hospital or health care facility where the provider is
7 a contractor of the hospital or facility.
8 3. It modifies the membership of the Maine Emergency Medical Services Commission
9 established in the bill by reducing the total number of members from 26 to 18 and
10 incorporates other changes to the membership of the commission.
11 4. It adds an appropriations and allocations section.

1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 20-A MRSA §4771, as amended by PL 2007, c. 240, Pt. VVV, §1, is further
3 amended to read:
4 §4771. Eligible institution and, semester and academic year; defined
5 As used in this chapter, unless the context otherwise indicates, "eligible institution"
6 means the institutions of the University of Maine System, the Maine Community College
7 System and the Maine Maritime Academy. "Semester" means the fall, spring or summer
8 term of an academic year. "Academic year" means one full academic cycle that includes a
9 summer semester, a fall semester and a spring semester.

26 SUMMARY
2277 This bill updates certain eligibility and academic requirements for secondary school
2288 students receiving a state subsidy for postsecondary courses. The bill also requires the
2299 Department of Education, in collaboration with eligible institutions, to determine annually
3300 a process for the reimbursement of tuition.

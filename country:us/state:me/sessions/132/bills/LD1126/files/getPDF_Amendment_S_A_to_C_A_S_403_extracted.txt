26 2. Firearm without a serial number. "Firearm without a serial number" means a

28 removed, obliterated or altered or a firearm not imprinted with an importer's or
29 manufacturer's serial number required by federal law. "Firearm without a serial number"

31 firearm that has been rendered permanently inoperable. For the purposes of this subsection,
32 "antique firearm" has the same meaning as in Title 15, section 394, subsection 1, paragraph

1 §2037. Violations
2 1. Unlawful possession. A person prohibited from possessing firearms under Title
3 15, section 393 who knowingly possesses a firearm without a serial number commits a
4 Class B crime.
5 2. Unlawful manufacture. A person prohibited from possessing firearms under Title
6 15, section 393 who manufactures a firearm without a serial number commits a Class B
7 crime.
8 3. Unlawful use. A person who uses a firearm without a serial number while
9 committing a Class A or Class B crime or while committing the crime of reckless conduct
10 under Title 17-A, section 211 commits a Class C crime.' '

13 SUMMARY
1144 This amendment replaces Committee Amendment "A" and the bill and changes the
1155 title of the bill. The amendment provides that it is a Class B crime for a person prohibited
1166 from possessing firearms under the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 15, section 393 to
1177 manufacture or knowingly possess a firearm without a serial number and that it is a Class
1188 C crime for a person to use a firearm without a serial number while committing a Class A
1199 or Class B crime or while committing the crime of reckless conduct. The amendment
2200 defines "firearm without a serial number" as a firearm that was not imprinted with a serial
2211 number by the importer or manufacturer in accordance with federal law or a firearm that
2222 has had the imprinted importer or manufacturer serial number removed, obliterated or
2233 altered. The amendment clarifies that firearms manufactured before 1969, antique firearms
2244 and firearms that are rendered permanently inoperable are excluded from the definition of
2255 "firearm without a serial number."

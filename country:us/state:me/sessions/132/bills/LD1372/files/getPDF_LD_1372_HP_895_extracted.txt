1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 5 MRSA §11117 is enacted to read:
3 §11117. Special Committee on Regulatory Review
4 1. Review of routine technical rules. Notwithstanding any provision of chapter 375
5 to the contrary, routine technical rules as described in section 8071, subsection 2 proposed
6 for adoption or amendment under chapter 375, subchapter 2-A are subject to review by the
7 Special Committee on Regulatory Review, established in this section for the purpose of
8 reviewing routine technical rules, referred to in this section as "the committee."
9 2. Committee membership; chairs. The committee consists of 6 members of the
10 Senate and 6 members of the House of Representatives, and the membership must be
11 evenly divided between each of the 2 parties holding the largest number of seats in each
12 chamber. The appointment of members and the selection of chairs are established by joint
13 rule of the Legislature.
14 3. Annual report. Beginning in 2028, the committee shall submit a report to the
15 Legislature listing the routine technical rules as described in section 8071, subsection 2,
16 sorted by agency, that were reviewed in the preceding calendar year and the action taken
17 by the committee on each reviewed rule. The report may also include the following:
18 A. Recommended legislation proposing changes to the process for the adoption of
19 routine technical rules; and
20 B. Recommendations for more effective collaboration among the Legislature, the
21 executive branch and the judicial branch with regard to adoption, enforcement and
22 adjudication of routine technical rules.
23 Sec. 2. Committee to adopt review procedure, recommend legislation. The
24 Special Committee on Regulatory Review, referred to in this section as "the committee"
25 and established in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, section 11117, shall establish a
26 process for the committee to review routine technical rules as described in Title 5, section
27 8071, subsection 2 proposed for adoption or amendment pursuant to Title 5, chapter 375,
28 subchapter 2-A. At a minimum, the process must allow for the committee to review
29 proposed routine technical rules for necessity, efficiency and a benefit of the rule to the
30 public and for the committee to approve that a rule be adopted with or without change or
31 to reject adoption of the rule. No later than February 1, 2026, the committee shall submit
32 legislation to the Second Regular Session of the 132nd Legislature proposing to amend
33 Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A as necessary to conform the statutory authority for the
34 adoption of routine technical rules with the review process established by the committee
35 pursuant to this section and to incorporate that review process into law.

H.P. 1357 House of Representatives, December 5, 2025
Resolve, to Rename a Bridge in Woolwich the Woolwich Veterans
Memorial Bridge
Approved for introduction by a majority of the Legislative Council pursuant to Joint Rule
203.
Received by the Clerk of the House on December 3, 2025. Referred to the Committee on
Transportation pursuant to Joint Rule 308.2 and ordered printed pursuant to Joint Rule 401.
ROBERT B. HUNT

1 Sec. 1. Bridge in Woolwich renamed. Resolved: That the Department of
2 Transportation shall designate Bridge 3039 on Main Street in the Town of Woolwich,
3 currently known as the Station 46 Bridge, the Woolwich Veterans Memorial Bridge.
4 SUMMARY
55 This resolve directs the Department of Transportation to designate Bridge 3039 on
66 Main Street in the Town of Woolwich the Woolwich Veterans Memorial Bridge.

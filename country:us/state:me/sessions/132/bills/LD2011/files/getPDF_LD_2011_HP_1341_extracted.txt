1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 24-A MRSA §4347, sub-§3, as enacted by PL 2019, c. 469, §8 and affected
3 by §9, is amended to read:
4 3. Carrier. "Carrier" has the same meaning as in section 4301‑A, subsection 3, except
5 that "carrier" does not include a multiple-employer welfare arrangement, as defined in
6 section 6601, subsection 5, if the multiple-employer welfare arrangement contracts with a
7 3rd-party administrator to manage and administer health benefits, including benefits for
8 prescription drugs. "Carrier" also includes the MaineCare program pursuant to Title 22,
9 chapter 855 and the group health plan provided to state employees and other eligible
10 persons pursuant to Title 5, section 285.
11 SUMMARY
1122 This bill removes the MaineCare program from the definition of "carrier" for the
1133 purposes of the law governing health plans that provide prescription drug benefits under
1144 the Maine Insurance Code.

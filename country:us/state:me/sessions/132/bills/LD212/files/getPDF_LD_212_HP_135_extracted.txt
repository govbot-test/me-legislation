1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 38 MRSA §634, sub-§5 is enacted to read:
3 5. Dam removal; energy valuation and alternative ownership. Notwithstanding
4 any provision of this subarticle to the contrary, the department may not approve an
5 application for a permit to remove a dam that is part of a hydropower project unless the
6 applicant:
7 A. Provides to the department an evaluation of the monetary value of the electrical or
8 mechanical power that the dam is capable of generating; and
9 B. Demonstrates to the department that the applicant made all reasonable efforts to
10 sell the dam and was unable to reach an agreement on a sale with an alternative owner.
11 The department may adopt rules to implement this subsection. Rules adopted pursuant to
12 this subsection are routine technical rules as defined in Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter
13 2-A.
14 SUMMARY
1155 This bill amends the law governing hydropower projects to require an applicant for a
1166 permit to remove a dam that is part of a hydropower project to provide to the Department
1177 of Environmental Protection or to the Maine Land Use Planning Commission, as
1188 applicable, an evaluation of the monetary value of the electrical or mechanical power that
1199 the dam is capable of generating. The applicant must also demonstrate to the department
2200 or commission that the applicant made all reasonable efforts to sell the dam and was unable
2211 to reach an agreement on a sale with an alternative owner. The department or commission
2222 is authorized to adopt routine technical rules to implement these requirements.

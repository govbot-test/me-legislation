Section 1:
132nd MAINE LEGISLATURE
SECOND REGULAR SESSION-2026
Legislative Document No. 2034
An Act to Support Funding for Educational Programs and
Opportunities
Submitted by the Department of Education pursuant to Joint Rule 203.
Received by the Clerk of the House on December 3, 2025. Referred to the Committee on
Education and Cultural Affairs pursuant to Joint Rule 308.2 and ordered printed pursuant to
Joint Rule 401.
ROBERT B. HUNT
Clerk
Presented by Representative BRENNAN of Portland.
Printed on recycled paper
1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 20-A MRSA §253, as amended by PL 2017, c. 466, §1, is further amended
3 by amending the section headnote to read:
4 §253. Commissioner's duties and powers
5 Sec. 2. 20-A MRSA §253, first ¶, as enacted by PL 1981, c. 693, §§5 and 8, is
6 amended to read:
7 The duties and powers of the commissioner shall be are as follows.
8 Sec. 3. 20-A MRSA §253, sub-§10 is enacted to read:
9 10. Support funding for educational programs and opportunities. The
10 commissioner may raise and expend private funds to support specific educational programs
11 and opportunities.
12 Sec. 4. 20-A MRSA §253, sub-§11 is enacted to read:
13 11. Promote public awareness. The commissioner may expend appropriated funds
14 for the purchase, production and distribution of promotional materials, including, but not
15 limited to, educational materials, branded merchandise and outreach materials, as part of
16 the department's efforts to support and promote public awareness and engagement related
17 to the department's programs, services and initiatives.
18 SUMMARY
1199 This bill authorizes the Commissioner of Education to raise and expend private funds
2200 for specific education programs and opportunities and spend appropriated funds on
2211 promotional materials.

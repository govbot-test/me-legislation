1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 20-A MRSA §1, sub-§23-C is enacted to read:
3 23-C. Public education. "Public education" means education that:
4 A. Is provided primarily at public expense;
5 B. Meets all state and federal requirements for education for enrolled students;
6 C. Is provided to students for free in every grade level from kindergarten to grade 12;
7 and
8 D. Is provided in accordance with section 1001, subsection 8.
9 Sec. 2. 20-A MRSA §2951, sub-§3, as amended by PL 1985, c. 797, §25, is further
10 amended to read:
11 3. Incorporated; located within State. Is incorporated under the laws of the State of
12 Maine or of the United States and, notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, if
13 the instructional facility and administrative offices are located within the State;
14 Sec. 3. 20-A MRSA §15689-F, sub-§3, as amended by PL 2013, c. 581, §11, is
15 further amended to read:
16 3. Casino revenues. If the annual funding for public education instruction for children
17 in public preschool programs and public education for children in kindergarten and grades
18 one to 12 is supported by casino revenues credited to the department pursuant to Title 8,
19 section 1036, the department shall journal expenditures from the General Purpose Aid for
20 Local Schools, General Fund account to the K-12 Essential Programs and Services, Other
21 Special Revenue Funds account to meet financial obligations and for purposes of cash flow.
22 SUMMARY
2233 This bill provides that a private school receiving approval for public funds for tuition
2244 purposes must be located within the State. The bill also defines the term "public education"
2255 and corrects a reference to the term.

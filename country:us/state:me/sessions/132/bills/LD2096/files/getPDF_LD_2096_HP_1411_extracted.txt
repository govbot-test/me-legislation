1 Emergency preamble. Whereas, acts and resolves of the Legislature do not
2 become effective until 90 days after adjournment unless enacted as emergencies; and
3 Whereas, agricultural fairs and the harness racing season will begin soon and this
4 legislation needs to take effect before the expiration of the 90-day period to allow the
5 change in the conditions of an agricultural fair license to occur as soon as possible; and
6 Whereas, in the judgment of the Legislature, these facts create an emergency within
7 the meaning of the Constitution of Maine and require the following legislation as
8 immediately necessary for the preservation of the public peace, health and safety; now,
9 therefore,
10 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:

32 SUMMARY
3333 This bill changes the definition of "agricultural fair" and prohibits an agricultural fair
3344 licensee from operating an agricultural fair outside of the assigned fair dates set by the
3355 Commissioner of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry and from advertising or
3366 marketing an agricultural fair as taking place outside of the assigned fair dates. The bill
3377 provides that a fair licensee may host an exhibition up to 24 hours prior to the assigned fair
3388 dates for the purposes of displaying and awarding prizes on nonlivestock competitions.
3399 The bill also provides that the commissioner may suspend or revoke the license of a
4400 licensee that violates these provisions.

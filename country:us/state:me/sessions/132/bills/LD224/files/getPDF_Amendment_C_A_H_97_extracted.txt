COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 147, L.D. 224
1 SUMMARY
22 This amendment, which is the majority report of the committee, changes references to
33 cyberattacks targeting state technology infrastructure, systems, services and critical
44 infrastructure to more broadly include such attacks that affect the State. It also removes
55 the requirement for a cyberattack to be in furtherance of political or social objectives to be
66 considered terrorism and makes other technical changes.
